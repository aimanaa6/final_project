Kaasp Bakery is more than just a bakery — it’s a celebration of colour, creativity, and community.

Founded by five friends with a shared passion for baking, Kaasp was born from the idea that desserts should not only taste amazing but also bring happiness with every bite. The name "Kaasp" is a blend of our founders’ initials, symbolising unity and friendship.

Inspired by the colours of the rainbow, we set out to create a space where each bake tells a story and every customer feels like family. From bright, fluffy cupcakes to layered celebration cakes bursting with colour, every creation is crafted with joy and love.

At Kaasp, community means everything. We love collaborating with local artists, hosting themed baking days, and celebrating the little moments that make life sweet. Our bakery is a place where creativity meets tradition — where new ideas are layered over time-honored recipes to create something truly special.

Whether you're picking up a cake, sipping one of our signature drinks, or simply browsing our rainbow of treats, we hope you feel the love, care, and passion that go into every corner of Kaasp Bakery.

Since opening our doors, Kaasp Bakery has grown into a local favourite, known for its bold themes, playful designs, and welcoming atmosphere. Whether you’re celebrating a big occasion or just stopping by for a treat, we’re here to add a little colour to your day.

Welcome to Kaasp — where every bite is a rainbow of joy.
