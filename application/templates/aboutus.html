Our Story

Kaasp Bakery started as a dream among friends — a dream to create bakes that spark joy, not just satisfy hunger. The name "Kaasp" is made from the initials of the founders, standing for friendship, unity, and shared purpose.

We believe that bakes should be as beautiful as they are tasty, which is why every product is made with attention to detail, love, and colour.
